# Introduction

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Once you've explored the documentation and are considering how this plugin could benefit your projects, you're welcome to pre-order it by reaching out to me at [hello@dasun.dev](mailto:hello@dasun.dev). I'm eager to assist you further and appreciate your interest.

Payoora is a developer-friendly payment gateway plugin that makes it easy to integrate [PayHere](https://payhere.lk) into your Laravel application. This plugin supports a variety of APIs to handle different payment processes:

- [Checkout API](https://support.payhere.lk/api-&-mobile-sdk/checkout-api)
- [Recurring API](https://support.payhere.lk/api-&-mobile-sdk/recurring-api)
- [Preapproval API](https://support.payhere.lk/api-&-mobile-sdk/preapproval-api)
- [Charging API](https://support.payhere.lk/api-&-mobile-sdk/charging-api)
- [Retrieval API](https://support.payhere.lk/api-&-mobile-sdk/retrieval-api)
- [Refund API](https://support.payhere.lk/api-&-mobile-sdk/refund-api)
- [Authorize API](https://support.payhere.lk/api-&-mobile-sdk/authorize-api)
- [Capture API](https://support.payhere.lk/api-&-mobile-sdk/capture-api)
- [Subscription Manager API](https://support.payhere.lk/api-&-mobile-sdk/subscription-manager-api)

With Payoora, you can easily manage everything from one-time payments to subscriptions and refunds, making payment processing simple and efficient for your users.
